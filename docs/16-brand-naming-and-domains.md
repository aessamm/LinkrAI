# 16 — Brand Naming and Domains

## 1. Official Product Name

The official product name is:

> **Linkrai**

Arabic display:

> **لينكراي**

## 2. Naming Rationale

Linkrai combines the meaning of:

- **Link** — the core content unit users save.
- **AI** — the intelligence layer that summarizes, categorizes, tags, and retrieves saved links.
- A possible spoken reading similar to **Link Ray**, suggesting that the product shines light on saved links and makes them easier to rediscover.

## 3. Official Writing Rules

Use this form in normal text:

```text
Linkrai
```

Do not use these as the default official name:

```text
LinkrAI
LinkRAI
Link AI
Linker AI
```

Exception:

- Logo or visual identity may emphasize the `AI` letters as a design treatment, but product text, docs, app store listing, and code references should use **Linkrai**.

## 4. Arabic Writing

Use:

```text
لينكراي
```

Avoid inconsistent alternatives unless explicitly needed for marketing testing.

## 7. Domain Candidates

The following domains were discussed as available candidates by the product owner at naming time:

```text
linkrai.app
linkrai.io
linkrai.link
linkrai.ai
```

Recommended priority:

1. `linkrai.app` — best for a mobile-first app.
2. `linkrai.ai` — best for AI positioning and SaaS credibility.
3. `linkrai.io` — good technical/SaaS fallback.
4. `linkrai.link` — good redirect, campaign, or playful domain.

## 8. Recommended Domain Strategy

If budget allows, reserve:

```text
linkrai.app
linkrai.ai
linkrai.io
```

Recommended primary options:

### Mobile-first positioning

```text
Primary: linkrai.app
Redirect: linkrai.ai and linkrai.io
```

### AI/SaaS positioning

```text
Primary: linkrai.ai
Redirect: linkrai.app and linkrai.io
```

## 9. Product Identity Direction

Linkrai should feel:

- Smart.
- Clear.
- Fast.
- Trustworthy.
- Premium.
- Modern.
- AI-native.
- Easy for Arabic and English users.

## 10. Brand Voice

Use direct, calm, helpful language.

Good examples:

```text
Saved to your Linkrai library.
Analyzing this link...
Summary is ready.
Search by meaning, not only keywords.
You reached your monthly summary limit.
```

Avoid exaggerated AI language.

Bad examples:

```text
Unlock the magical infinite AI brain of the internet.
```

## 11. Legal and Availability Checks

Before public launch, complete:

- Domain purchase.
- App Store name availability.
- Google Play name availability.
- Trademark search in target markets.
- Social media handle search.

Do not assume legal availability from domain availability alone.
